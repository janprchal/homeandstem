## What to Look for in a Closet Organizer

### What kind of storage you're actually missing

Hanging rod free but no shelves? A rod-hung organizer solves it. Shelves but no dividers?
Acrylic dividers are the cheaper fix. Diagnose the real gap before buying.

### Weight rating

Fabric organizers are rated for folded clothes and light items, not dense stacks of books or
heavy boots — check the rating before loading one up.

### Tool-free vs. assembly

Hanging and divider organizers need zero tools. Drawer units and rolling racks need basic
assembly but offer more structure in return.

<CalloutBox variant="tip" title="Small-closet tip">
  Measure your closet rod's free length before ordering a hanging organizer — most need at
  least 12–14 inches of clear rod space, and a tight fit makes the shelves sag forward.
</CalloutBox>
