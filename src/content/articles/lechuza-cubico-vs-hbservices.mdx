## Reservoir and watering interval

The Cubico's larger reservoir and gauge mean genuinely less fuss — about two weeks between
refills versus roughly five days for the HBServices in the same conditions.

## Price and value

If you're potting up a whole balcony, the HBServices' price adds up in your favor. For one
or two statement plants, the Cubico is worth it.
