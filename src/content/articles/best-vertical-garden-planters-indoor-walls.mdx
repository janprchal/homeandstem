## What to Look for in a Vertical Garden Planter

### Felt pockets vs. rigid panels

Felt pockets are cheap and flexible but need more careful watering. Rigid panels like
Florafelt cost more but hold moisture more predictably and look more like a true living wall.

### Indoor vs. outdoor rating

Not every wall planter is rated for outdoor humidity and temperature swings — check before
mounting one on an exposed balcony wall.

### Drainage protection for the wall behind it

Look for a waterproof backing layer, or plan to mount over a surface that can handle
occasional drips.

<CalloutBox variant="tip" title="Mounting tip">
  Hang felt pocket planters with a slight forward tilt rather than flush against the wall —
  it keeps runoff from soaking the wall behind the lower pockets.
</CalloutBox>
