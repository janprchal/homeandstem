## What to Look for in an Apartment-Friendly Houseplant

### Light tolerance

Most apartments don't have the bright, unobstructed light a plant tag photo implies. Favor
plants explicitly rated for low-to-medium indirect light unless you have a clear south- or
west-facing window.

### Watering forgiveness

A plant that bounces back from a missed week is worth more than a fussier one with slightly
better air-cleaning stats. Snake plants and ZZ plants are the most forgiving on this list.

### Pet safety

If cats or dogs share the apartment, check toxicity before falling for a plant on looks alone
— spider plants are the only fully pet-safe pick here.

<CalloutBox variant="tip" title="Apartment tip">
  Group two or three plants together on a tray instead of spreading them around the room —
  it raises humidity slightly around the leaves and makes watering day a single five-minute
  task instead of five separate ones.
</CalloutBox>
