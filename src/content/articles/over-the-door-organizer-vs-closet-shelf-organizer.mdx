## Storage capacity and what each one actually holds

The Gorilla Grip's 24 mesh pockets are genuinely flexible — reviewers use them for shoes,
cleaning supplies, hair tools, and kids' toys — but every pocket is the same flat shape. The
Pipishell's six shelves plus three drawers handle a wider range of item shapes, including
folded sweaters and bulkier items the door organizer's pockets can't fit.

## Installation and where each one uses your space

This is the real trade-off. The Gorilla Grip uses door space you might already need for a
mirror or robe hook, installing in about a minute with four over-the-door hooks. The
Pipishell uses closet rod space instead, hanging from two hook loops — a better deal if your
door is already busy, but only if you have rod space to spare.
