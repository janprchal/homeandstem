## What to Look for in a Self-Watering Planter

### Reservoir size

Bigger reservoirs mean longer gaps between watering — important on an exposed balcony.

### Water-level indicator

A visible gauge takes the guesswork out of refills and prevents overwatering.

### Material and UV resistance

Balcony planters live in direct sun. Look for UV-stable resin that won't fade or crack.
